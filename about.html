About Me

Data Engineer turned Software Engineer — building at the intersection of both.

I started my career as a Data Engineer. I quickly learned that the most impactful data work happens when you bring a software engineering mindset to it — clean abstractions, testable code, reliable deployments, and thoughtful system design. I'm happiest at the intersection of data and software craftsmanship.

Outside of work, I'm drawn to health and well-being, philosophy, and history. I prefer deep, meaningful conversations over surface-level small talk — the kind that challenge ideas and leave you thinking long after.

What Drives Me

Engineering Rigour

Data systems deserve the same standards as any production software — version control, CI/CD, testing, and observability.

Scale-First Thinking

I think in terms of throughput, latency, and fault tolerance from day one, not as an afterthought.

Product Mindset

Pipelines and platforms are products too. I care about the developer experience and the end-user impact.
